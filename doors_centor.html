For all door types up to 15’ 3 ⅜” (4657mm) wide, you can choose between any of the two fabric combinations, one housed in either doorjamb. Two Combinations

For Centor Integrated Doors spanning between 15’ 3 ⅜” (4657mm) and 18’ 11 ½” (5780mm), you may also choose between a screen or a shade, in one fabric type that meets along the opening.
